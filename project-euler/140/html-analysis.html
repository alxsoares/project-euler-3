Project Euler #140: Modified Fibonacci Golden Nuggets

Link to Project Euler problem No. 137

\[ s = \sqrt{5} \\ a = (1+s)/2 \\ b = (1-s)/2 \\ G[n] = \frac{1}{s} \cdot \left\{ a^{n-2}\left(4a+1\right)+b^{n-2}\left(-4b-1\right) \right\} \]

Therefore: [ Given limits according to the |q| < 1 ]

\[ A_G(x) = \frac{1}{s} \cdot \left\{ \dfrac{ (4a+1)a^{-1}x }{1 - ax} + \dfrac{ (-4b-1)b^{-1}x }{1-bx}\right\} \\ A_G(x) = \frac{1}{s} \cdot \left\{ \dfrac{ (4+a^{-1})x }{1 - ax} + \dfrac{ (-4-b^{-1})x }{1-bx}\right\} \\ A_G(x) = \frac{x}{s} \cdot \left\{ \dfrac{ (4+a^{-1}) }{1 - ax} + \dfrac{ (-4-b^{-1}) }{1-bx}\right\} \\ A_G(x) = \frac{x}{s} \cdot \left\{ \dfrac{ (4+\left(\frac{1+s}{2}\right)^{-1}) }{1 - \left(\frac{1+s}{2}\right) \cdot x} + \dfrac{ (-4-\left(\frac{1-s}{2}\right)^{-1}) }{1-\left(\frac{1-s}{2}\right)x}\right\} \]
